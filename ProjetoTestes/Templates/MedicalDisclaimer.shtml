This report shows the results of your Equifit and compares your measurements against average ratings. These ratings are based on publicly available measurement charts and are provided for informational purposes only — your measurements may vary as a result of your age, gender and fitness level. Your Equifit is intended to establish baselines to use in working with you to establish your fitness goals and measuring your progress in achieving those goals. It is not intended for use determining the state of your health or in the diagnosis of any medical conditions and is not a substitute for a professional medical evaluation.

We advise you to see your physician on a regular basis and to seek their advice if you have any questions or concerns regarding the medical implications of your Equifit results.
